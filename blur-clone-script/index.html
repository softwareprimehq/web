How does a Blur clone script Function

Step-by-Step Breakdown of Our Blur Clone Script

Wallet Connection

Your users will securely connect their crypto wallets (MetaMask, Wallet Connect, Coinbase Wallet, OKX Wallet). The marketplace verifies wallet addresses and access permissions, to seamlessly manage and access their NFTs within your marketplace.

Discover and Explore

Users connect their wallets to filter and explore NFTs by categories, price, rarity, collection, or blockchain. They can easily browse collections, view details, and refine searches to find NFTs that match specific criteria.

Listing NFT

To sell NFTs, users select items from their wallets, and set prices, royalty percentages, and listing durations. They can upload multiple NFTs using batch listing with predefined settings for streamlined sales.

Buy NFT

Buyers browse NFTs, choose based on price and rarity, and place bids or make outright purchases using wallet funds. NFTs are securely transferred to the buyer’s wallet for a transparent transaction.

Sell NFT

Sellers can set fixed prices or conduct auctions for their NFTs. Once a transaction is confirmed, the NFT’s ownership is updated on the blockchain, ensuring a seamless transfer process.

NFT Lending

Users can create offers specifying loan amounts, interest rates, and minimum durations. Borrowers can borrow NFTs by agreeing to these terms, with collateral ensuring NFT recovery in case of default.

Ownership Transfer

Whether buying, selling, or lending, NFT ownership is smoothly transferred on the blockchain. It ensures that your users receive their assets directly into their connected wallets after the transaction is finalized.

Get Rewards

To encourage active participation, our script offers rewards in the form of native tokens, exclusive NFTs, and community incentives. Users earn rewards for trading, listing, holding, and contributing to the marketplace’s growth.
