Our Workflow

Working Mechanism of our IEO Solutions

Understand How Our IEO Development Solutions Works

Applying the Project

Users apply by submitting their details through a Google form.

Verifying the Project

Admin verifies the project details, and white paper to launch the project on the launchpad.

Listing the Project

Admin lists the project on the platform to initiate the fundraising process.

Holding Period

Users hold their tokens for a set duration that may be days, hours, or minutes and the average is calculated.

Subscription Period

Investors commit their funds based on a single initial investment(minimum commitment level) and calculated average(maximum commitment limit).

Calculation Period

Their assets are locked, and the calculation period begins to determine the hard cap per user. The hard cap ensures fair token distribution. If a user reaches the hardcap, any extra tokens are reallocated among others based on their commitment ratio.

Token Allocation and Distribution

Once the final allocation is set, the Admin deducts the corresponding amount from each user's locked balance. Tokens are issued based on the hardcap limit, and any extra commitment is refunded. The remaining crypto and tokens are credited to the user's wallet.
