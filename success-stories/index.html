Get It All Delivery Powers Up the Future of On-Demand Delivery

Overview

Get It All Delivery is an emerging multi-category delivery platform launched in January 2023. It is designed to connect licensed food outlets, grocery stores, and alcohol retailers with customers through a compliant, secure, and user-friendly solution.

Faced with the challenge of launching in industries with complex delivery regulations, the company partnered with a reliable development team to create a scalable and customizable platform.

The result?

A fully operational system that positions Get It All Delivery for long-term success in a highly competitive on-demand delivery market.

The Challenge

Before launching, Get It All Delivery sought a tech partner capable of building a robust delivery app tailored to multiple regulated sectors like alcohol and food. The primary obstacles included:

  • Navigating varied regulations for food, groceries, cannabis and alcohol delivery
  • Finding a team with deep domain experience in multi-service delivery
  • Launching quickly without compromising customization or functionality
  • Creating an intuitive, unified platform for customers, vendors, and delivery drivers

The Solution

After reviewing several tech providers, Get It All Delivery chose a Trioangle with proven experience in on-demand delivery solutions. The team provided:

  • A fully functional, multi-interface delivery platform
  • Essential features like order tracking, real-time alerts, secure payments, and compliance-ready workflows
  • A scalable infrastructure ready for future growth and regional expansion
  • A flexible platform combining pre-built stability with custom features

The Results

Get It All Delivery launched its beta version, onboarding restaurants, grocery stores, alcohol vendors, drivers, and customers. Early results show:

  • A seamless and intuitive experience across all user roles
  • Real-time orders and secure, compliant delivery transactions
  • Faster partner onboarding and positive early user feedback
  • A strong tech foundation supporting business expansion

Measurable Impact

Though still in beta and not disclosing revenue publicly, Get It All Delivery has:
