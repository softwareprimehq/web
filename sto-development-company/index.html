What is Security Token Offering?

A Security Token Offering (STO) is a fundraising method where real-world assets or equity are tokenized and sold to investors. STOs are fully regulated, ensuring legal compliance and investor protection. They use blockchain technology and adhere to regulations to make investments safer and more transparent.

Security Token Offering (STO) is a way for companies to raise capital by creating tokenized securities on a blockchain. The security tokens can represent various assets, including debt, equity, or asset-based investment.

Security Token Offerings (STOs) are a more secure fundraising option than Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) and Initial Exchange Offerings (IEOs) because they are backed by regulated assets and comply with financial regulations.

STOs provide complete regulatory compliance, enhanced security, options for fractional ownership, access for global investors, and better liquidity by trading tokenized assets on regulated exchanges.
