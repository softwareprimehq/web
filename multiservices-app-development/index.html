What is a Multi-Service App?

A multi-service app handles multiple services, including food delivery, parcel delivery, grocery delivery, pharmacy delivery, etc, in a single platform. It simplifies the operations effectively for the user to obtain their needs in one place.

Multiple Currency

Schedule Ride

Handicap Seat Accessibility
