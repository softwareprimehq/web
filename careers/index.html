Opening For Content Writer

Position & Summary

We are looking for a content writer who will be responsible for creating compelling, high-quality content that aligns with the company’s brand voice and marketing goals. The purpose of the Content Writer position is to enhance the company’s online presence and visibility by producing well-researched, SEO-friendly content that attracts and retains a clearly defined audience.

By creating engaging and informative content, the Content Writer will help increase brand awareness, boost organic traffic, improve search engine rankings, and support overall marketing objectives.

Roles Responsibilities

  • Research and organise sources and information.
  • Collaborate and brainstorm with the team to develop new strategies and ideas.
  • Write well-researched, keyword-driven content to boost organic traffic.
  • Create optimised and engaging title tags and meta descriptions to increase click-through rates.
  • Develop clear and innovative headlines and body copy.
  • Produce high-quality blog posts on industry-relevant topics, addressing user queries in detail.
  • Write content on various topics for podcasts, e-books, websites, blogs, social media, case studies, whitepapers, banners, and more related to App Development and Blockchain Solutions.
  • Create, execute, and maintain a content calendar.
  • Ensure consistency in writing style, fonts, images, and tone.
  • Collaborate with designers and developers to align written content with the brand.
  • Edit and proofread content produced by other team members.

Qualification

  • Any Degree

Experience

  • 0 To 1 Years Of Content Writing Experience

Job Location

  • Madurai

Employment Type

  • Full-time, Permanent

Start Date

  • Immediate
