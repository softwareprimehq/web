Understanding the Workflow of Axie Infinity Clone Script

The Core Process of Setting Up Your NFT Gaming Platform

Step 1

Your users must connect to a Ronin Wallet to access the marketplace. After connecting the wallet, fund it with ETH or $RON. The user signs in, and their Axie Infinity account is automatically linked to their wallet.

Step 2

Once logged in, your users can browse various Axie NFTs, land plots, and other digital collections available for sale. They can filter by traits, price, and other criteria to find the assets they're interested in.

Step 3

Users can review an Axie’s stats, click "Buy Now" to proceed, and confirm the transaction via their Ronin Wallet. Alternatively, make custom offers or add multiple Axies and items to their cart for seamless purchases.

Step 4

Once ready to purchase, users review order details and associated fees on the checkout page. To confirm, they must sign a transaction on their Ronin wallet, authorizing the purchase and fund transfer.

Step 5

Before finalizing the purchase, the user will be prompted to approve the transaction through their connected wallet. The user signs the transaction from within their wallet app to confirm the purchase​.

Step 6

Once the user confirms the transaction, the platform verifies the payment (in the form of Ethereum, Ronin, or another token) through the blockchain. The blockchain records the transaction, confirming the ownership transfer of the asset.

Step 7

After the payment is confirmed, the purchased Axie or asset is transferred to the buyer's wallet. This process is usually done through the Ronin network for Axie Infinity, ensuring low transaction costs and fast execution.

Step 8

Users can view their transaction history and purchased assets will appear in their inventory. User can now manage their assets, including breeding Axies, listing for sale, or transferring them to other users.
