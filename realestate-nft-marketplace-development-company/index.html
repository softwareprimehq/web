User Login and Tokenization

Users can connect wallets and sign up to enter the virtual real estate market. Choose a blockchain network and token standard to tokenize physical properties into NFTs, representing full or fractional ownership of a specific property.

Step 1

Listing and Discovery

When tokenizing, creators provide details like Ownership details, location, category, property info, minting options, legal docs, Leasing, and renting terms. Sellers can set or update pricing. Buyers can browse listings with search and filter tools to find suitable properties.

Step 2

Purchase

Once properties are listed, buyers can purchase their desired property at a fixed price or through bidding. With bidding, buyers can place offers, allowing the highest bidder to acquire the property or a fraction of it, creating an engaging and dynamic buying experience.

Step 3

Secure Transactions

When a buyer shows interest, they initiate a transaction, transferring funds directly to the seller's wallet. Smart contracts automate payment verification, ownership transfer, and legal compliance. This eliminates intermediaries, ensuring a seamless process.

Step 4

Ownership Transfer

After the transaction, the digital signature (ownership) is transferred to the buyer. This transfer is recorded on the blockchain, ensuring a secure and transparent ownership history. The buyerâ€™s digital signature serves as proof of property ownership.

Step 5

Access & Manage Property
