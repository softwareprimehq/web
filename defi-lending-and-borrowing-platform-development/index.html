Working Process of DeFi Lending

How Does DeFi Lending Work?

  • Lenders supply capital into liquidity pools for borrowers to use.
  • They set the conditions for the loan, including interest rates and collateral
  • Lenders ask for collateral to secure the loan
  • Then the transaction is processed through the smart contract.
  • Finally, they receive the loan amount and interest after repayment.

Working Process of DeFi Borrowing

How Does DeFi Borrowing Work?

  • Borrowers apply for a loan and choose a lender based on the terms offered.
  • Accepts loan terms before signing the agreement.
  • They provide collateral to lenders to guarantee the loan amount.
  • Repay the loan with interest as agreed.
  • Borrowers receive collateral after the loan is fully paid back with interest.
