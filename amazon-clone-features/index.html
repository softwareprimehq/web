CliqBuy- User

Explore The Smart Features For Seamless Shopping

Wishlist

Users can save their favourite items to a wishlist for future purchases. This allows them to simply revisit, access, and buy these items at anytime without the hassle of searching all over again.

Compare

Users can compare similar products side by side, evaluating differences in brand, price, reviews, and other specs. This streamlined view supports better decision-making and helps ensure they choose the most suitable product.

Add to cart

This feature allows users to select products and save them in the cart. It enhances the shopping experience by providing a convenient way to manage, review, and modify items before proceeding to checkout.

Support ticket- User

Users can easily create support tickets to report issues or ask questions about payment completion and other concerns. The platform reviews the ticket and provides a prompt response to users through smooth communication.

Manage delivery address

Users can add, edit, or remove addresses at their convenience. This feature allows them to manage multiple addresses, ensuring accurate deliveries and providing flexibility for timely and hassle-free service.

User-side bulk ordering

This feature allows customers to place bulk orders of their preferred products in specified maximum quantities. It offers convenience, quick delivery, and cost savings by processing large orders efficiently.

Manage Tax- User

Users can view applicable taxes on their selected product before completing the order, ensuring transparency. It guarantees accurate tax calculations and seamless compliance with local tax regulations.

Pickup order from pickup point

Users can choose a designated pickup point to collect their orders at their convenience. This ensures quicker handovers, reduces delivery delays, and provides flexibility for both customers and delivery partners.

Message merchant (Web)

Buyers can directly message merchants through the web for inquiries, product updates, custom requests, and order details. This real-time messaging option improves communication, builds trust, and enhances overall user satisfaction.

Advanced search filters

Advanced search filters enable users to refine search results based on specific criteria, such as category, price, ratings, and more. This helps them quickly discover correct products and increase the search experience.

Flash deals

Users can access limited-time flash deals on various products while grabbing discounts and saving money. This encourages quick purchase decisions, boosts user engagement, conversion rates and overall sales effectively.

Coupon code

Customers can simply apply the coupon code at checkout to unlock exclusive discounts. The minimum amount is reduced by the coupon and helping them enjoy significant savings on their purchase.

CliqBuy- Merchant

Powerful Features To Grow, Sell, And Succeed

Shop Register & Management

Merchants can easily register their shop by updating the essential details like name, address, phone, state, city, and more. They can also link their social media accounts to boost shop visibility.

Product Management (Add, Edit & Delete)

This allows merchants to manage their inventory by adding new products, updating existing product details, or deleting unavailable items. It enhances up-to-date listings through a user-friendly platform interface.

Manage Tax for product

Merchants can control the tax settings for their products by specifying applicable tax rates based on product categories or regions. It helps merchants stay compliant with local tax regulations.

Manage Product Variations - Attributes & Colors for Products

Merchants organize product variations, including specific attributes and the color of the products. This allows them to offer multiple options for each product, giving customers more choices and enhancing the shopping experience.

Flexible Bulk Ordering & Minimum Quantity Control

Enable flexible bulk ordering by setting minimum quantity requirements for products. This feature allows merchants to control order volumes, support wholesale transactions, and ensure efficient inventory management for larger purchases.

Manage Order Pickup points

Merchants can define specific pickup point locations, allowing customers to choose where to collect their orders during checkout. This helps streamline logistics and improves flexibility.

Order Management (View and Update Order Status)

Manage orders by viewing detailed order information and updating order statuses in real-time. This ensures smooth operations, faster deliveries, and improves customer satisfaction through efficient order processing.

Conversation with buyer(Web)

Merchants can engage in real-time web conversations with buyers for order updates, clarifications, and support. It will enhance platform transparency, customer retention, and faster resolution of queries.

Withdraw request for payment

Enable merchants to request the withdrawal of their earned income. The platform processes and sends payment to their bank account or other payment methods. This ensures seamless, secure, and timely transactions.

CliqBuy- Admin

Manage Everything With These Advanced Features

Dashboard

A dashboard is a centralized platform offering real-time insights into key metrics like total consumers, merchants, product categories, inventory, and completed orders, enabling quick decision-making and performance tracking for businesses.

Manage Sub-Admin users and their permissions (RBAC)

Admin can add sub-admins and assign specific roles to oversee purchasing operations, ensuring efficient and smooth platform management with proper delegation of responsibilities and access control.

Manage Merchants (Commission rates, Owe amount, verification form)

Admin can add and verify merchant accounts, set commission rates, track owed amounts, approve payout requests, process payouts, and ensure financial accuracy for seamless operations and effective merchant management.

Product Management - Inhouse & seller products

Admin can manage product listings, oversee inventory, set pricing, and handle orders for both in-house and seller products. They ensure smooth operations and optimize product availability.

Bulk Import of Products

This feature allows the admin to upload large volumes of products quickly. It simplifies inventory management by reducing manual data entry and ensuring a more streamlined and organized product catalog.

Order Management - In-house & Merchant orders

The admin can view all merchant orders and update the status of in-house orders. This improves efficient tracking and management of both merchant and in-house order processes on the platform.

User Management - View, Ban & Delete

Admin can view user profiles, ban users by restricting access, and delete accounts permanently. This functionality ensures control over the platform, maintaining security, and managing users based on violations or inactivity.

Manage Category

Admin can add, edit, delete, and organize product categories by assigning names, order level, banner images, and icons. This enhances user visibility and helps maintain a structured listing.

Manage Attributes

Establish clear and consistent product attribute names and values to ensure accurate and comprehensive listings. This increases search exposure, consumer knowledge, and conversions by making product details easier to compare and filter.

Manage Color

Manage product colors by assigning diverse color names and their corresponding color codes to ensure visual consistency across your platform. This helps improve branding, customer experience, and ease of navigation for users.

Manage Brand

Allows admins to add or edit brand names, logos, meta titles, and descriptions. This will enable efficient organization of product listings and improve search visibility for better user experience and categorization.

Manage Tax

Set up and customize tax types to guarantee precise billing and legal compliance. This proper tax setting supports transparent transactions and enhances the overall reliability of the platform.

Pickup point management

Admin can add and manage multiple pickup locations for deliveries. This ensures efficient route planning, accurate scheduling, and smooth coordination between customers and delivery teams.

Support Ticket

Admin can view customer support tickets, track their status, and send replies to address concerns. It will enhance communication and effective issue resolution between users and the platform.

Manage Flash Deal

Admin can add new flash deals on the products by entering the deal title, selecting a color, uploading a banner image, setting a discount from the base price, and defining the start and end dates for the promotion.

Manage Coupon Code

This feature allows admin to create, edit, and delete coupon codes for discounts or promotions. It includes setting expiry dates, usage limits, and associating codes with specific products types.

Manage shipping cities

Admin can add the city name, state, country, and cost to manage shipping cities. This flexibility allows consumers to specify delivery options and choose where their items can be shipped.

View Newsletter Subscribers & Send Email

Admins can view newsletter subscribers' details and send product updates or promotional emails to users and merchants. This will keep them informed about new features, offers, or important news efficiently through the dashboard.

Manage Reports

Admin can manage detailed reports including merchant and in-house orders, user searches, product stock levels, wishlists, and commission history. This strengthens complete oversight of platform activities and performance metrics in one place.
