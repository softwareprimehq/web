How Does Our Foundation Clone Script Work?

Step 1:

Connect Your Wallet

To get started on Foundation, users must connect a digital wallet (like MetaMask) to the platform. This wallet will store the NFTs and facilitate all transactions, from minting to purchasing.

Step 2:

Minting an NFT

Once connected, creators can mint their digital art as NFTs by uploading their files to the platform. The process involves creating a unique token linked to the artwork. A minting fee is applied during this process.

Step 3:

Listing NFTs for Sale

After minting, creators can list their NFTs for auction or fixed price. Listings include detailed descriptions and an auction timer. NFTs are visible to potential buyers within the marketplace.

Step 4:

Bidding or Purchasing

Buyers can either place bids on auctioned NFTs or purchase them directly if listed at a fixed price. The auction goes to the highest bidder, if not, the sale is finalized at the stated price.

Step 5:

Transaction

Once the purchase is complete, the NFT’s ownership is transferred to the buyer. The transaction is processed through the blockchain, and the payment is sent to the creator’s wallet after deducting a marketplace fee.

Step 6:

Creator Royalties on Resale

It ensures that creators receive royalties on secondary sales. Whenever the NFT is resold, the original creator earns a percentage of the resale price. Smart contracts handle this automatically.

Step 7:

Final Ownership Transfer

Following a successful transaction, the NFT gets moved to the purchaser's wallet, with the ownership being registered on the blockchain, guaranteeing transparency and security. The buyer now holds the verifiable proof of ownership.
