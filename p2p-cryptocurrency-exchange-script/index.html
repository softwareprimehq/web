Identity Check

Using ID Analyzer, our system automatically verifies the documents uploaded by your users. This enables only legitimate traders to access your platform.

Users download Google Authenticator to enable 2FA. It generates OTPs to secure user accounts. Even if hackers compromise passwords, they still protect them.

A unique signature that is defined by the user and that is received for each email. It helps detect phishing attempts. This builds trust by ensuring users can verify authentic communications.

Your P2P Crypto Exchange Script automatically logs your users out after a set period of inactivity. This prevents unauthorized access to accounts left open and unattended.

Your users can view all active devices with location, date, and IP address in their account settings. The delete option removes the record and logs the user out from that device.

Allows your users to withdraw funds only from the addresses whitelisted. This gives protection against unauthorized withdrawals.

Account Closure

One-Step Withdrawal
