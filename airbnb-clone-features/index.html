In case of a dispute, that lasts longer than 14 days, administrators intervene to make fair and unbiased decisions. They offer mediation or advice to help solve the issue. This establishes stronger trust between hosts and users.

Manage Offer (Admin)

Platform administrators can launch and execute platform-wide promotional campaigns, including seasonal sales, time-limited deals, and holiday promotions.
