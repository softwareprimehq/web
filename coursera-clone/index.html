Course Listing Fee

The course listing fee is a one-time or recurring charge imposed on the instructor to list their courses on the platform. It covers administrative costs associated with course management, marketing, and platform maintenance.

Premium Courses

You can make money by offering premium courses that consist of multimedia content material, personalized tutor support, and additional resources not available in standard courses. Students pay a premium price to enroll in these courses, which provides a significant revenue stream for instructors and the platform.

In-App Promo Fee

The In-App Promo Fee is a charge levied on instructors for promoting their courses within your e-learning platform's ecosystem. It’s a win-win revenue-making strategy. You will earn additional revenue with this in-app promo fee. Instructors get increased course visibility among students, potentially leading to higher enrollment rates and revenue.

Via Google Ads

You can make income via Google Ads by displaying advertisements on the platform, typically through Google's AdSense program. Advertisers pay to display their ads, and the platform earns revenue either through cost per click (CPC) or cost per impressions (CPM).
