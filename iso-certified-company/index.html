Trioangle Technologies Now ISO Certified Company

Trioangle Technologies certified to ISO 9001:2015 and ISO/IEC 27001:2013 for Quality Management and Information Security Management Systems in developing web and mobile software and offering services to all verticals of online business.

December 07, 2021- Trioangle Minds Technologies proudly announced that it has achieved ISO 9001:2015 and ISO/IEC 27001:2013 certification. Achieving this certificate shows its commitment to satisfy customers with high-quality app platforms with continuous efforts.

With the objective to provide products and services as per customer needs governed by the regulatory framework, to enhance customer satisfaction, ISO 9001 is a quality management standard framed by the International Organization for Standardization. Mainly, it is an association of both government and private organizations situated in many countries.

ISO 9001:2015 standard is mainly utilized with the focus on continually providing services to satisfy the customers and the involvement of employees in such processes.

ISO/IEC 27001:2013 defines the requirements to establish, implement and improve the Information Security Management System (ISMS) within the context of the organization.

Commenting on this certification, P.Vignesh, CEO happily said “I am in extreme happiness with the performance of everyone who is behind this achievement. Beyond their work, showing a commitment towards client satisfaction via the quality of what they do is inspiring for me. ISO certification is one big milestone of our journey. Still more to do. We will continue our services and products to customers without any compromise on quality.”

Trioangle Minds Technology was certified to ISO 9001:2015 standard by a performance review from Breakthrough Management Quality Registrar(BMQR) is not for any profit, affiliate. This is only a motivation for the mission and commitment to providing stand-out solutions for any kind of business. The certificate of registration is available on Trioangle’s website.

Yet another level is stepped into it. Yes! For an organization, Information Security Management Systems(ISMS) is highly essential. This proves how a sensitive data manager manages in a secure way. ISO now certifies our ISMS works. With the active working of the system admin team, Trioangle is now also referred to as ISO/IEC 27001:2013 certified company. This will be a great driving force to move ahead in our process.
